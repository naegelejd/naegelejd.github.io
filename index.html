About

I am a software developer employed by the Functional MRI Facility at the National Institute of Mental Health. My work consists of website development, database design, development of medical image reconstruction software, and construction of various end-user command-line tools.

In my spare time I enjoy studying programming language design and runtime implementations. I also like learning new programming languages and APIs, experimenting with embedded systems, and hacking on website backends.
